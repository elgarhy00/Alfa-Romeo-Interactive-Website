About Tonale Plug-in Hybrid

The Tonale Plug-in Hybrid is the most powerful and electrified model in the Tonale lineup, offering sporty performance and efficiency.

Specifications

  • Power: 280 HP
  • Engine: 1.3-liter MultiAir turbo gasoline + electric motor
  • Acceleration: 0-100 km/h in 6.2 seconds
  • Battery: 15.5 kWh
  • Electric Range: Up to 82 km (city), 69 km (combined)
  • Drivetrain: Q4 all-wheel drive
  • Charging: Full charge in 2.5 hours with 7.4 kW fast charger

This model delivers unparalleled control and driving pleasure, making it the sportiest and most efficient Plug-in Hybrid C-SUV in its class.
