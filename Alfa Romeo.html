Frequently Asked Questions

What is the warranty period for Alfa Romeo vehicles?

Alfa Romeo offers a standard warranty of 3 years or 100,000 km, whichever comes first, covering manufacturing defects and repairs.

How often should I service my Alfa Romeo?

We recommend servicing your Alfa Romeo every 15,000 km or once a year, depending on your driving conditions and model specifications.

Can I customize my Alfa Romeo before purchase?

Yes, Alfa Romeo offers a range of customization options, including exterior colors, interior trims, and performance packages. Contact your dealer for details.
