About Giulia

The Alfa Romeo Giulia offers a perfect balance of performance and efficiency with its high-performance engines and innovative design. The Giulia's chassis and suspension design ensure a dynamic and responsive driving experience, ideal for enthusiasts.

Specifications

  • Engines: Diesel and petrol, 160 HP to 280 HP
  • Drivetrain: Rear-wheel drive
  • Suspension: Innovative systems for optimal handling
  • Weight/Power Ratio: Optimized for performance

Frequently Asked Questions

What engine options are available for the Giulia?

The Alfa Romeo Giulia offers diesel and petrol engines ranging from 160 HP to 280 HP, with the Quadrifoglio model boasting a 510 HP V6 engine.

What is the warranty period for the Giulia?

The Giulia comes with a standard 3-year warranty or 100,000 km, whichever comes first, with extended options available.

Does the Giulia support advanced driver assistance systems?

Yes, the Giulia includes features like adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, and automated emergency braking on select models.
