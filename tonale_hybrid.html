About Tonale Hybrid

The Alfa Romeo Tonale Hybrid combines efficiency and performance with its advanced diesel engine and cutting-edge technology.

Specifications

  • Engine: 1.6-liter 130hp diesel
  • Torque: 320 Nm at 1500 rpm
  • Transmission: 6-speed Alfa Romeo TCT dual-clutch automatic
  • Steering: Most direct in segment (13:6:1 ratio)
  • Braking: Integrated Brake System (IBS)
  • Features: Torque Vectoring, Frequency Selective Damping (FSD) shock absorbers
  • Suspension: MacPherson scheme on both axles

The Tonale Hybrid offers exceptional agility and driving dynamics, making it a top choice in its class. Its precise steering and advanced suspension system ensure a safe and comfortable driving experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the fuel efficiency of the Tonale Hybrid?

The Tonale Hybrid achieves up to 5.2 L/100 km (combined cycle) with its 1.6-liter diesel engine.

What is the warranty period for the Tonale Hybrid?

It comes with a 3-year warranty or 100,000 km, with extended options available.

Does it include advanced driver assistance systems?

Yes, it features adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, and automated emergency braking.
