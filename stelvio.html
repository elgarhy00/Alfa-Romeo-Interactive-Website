Frequently Asked Questions

What engine options are available for the Stelvio?

The Stelvio offers 2.0L Turbo Petrol engines (200-280 HP) and 2.2L Diesel engines (160-210 HP), all paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission.

What is the warranty period for the Stelvio?

It comes with a 3-year warranty or 100,000 km, with extended options available.

Does the Stelvio include advanced driver assistance systems?

Yes, it features adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, and automated emergency braking on select models.
